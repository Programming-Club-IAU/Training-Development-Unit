Your Flutter app should include:

1. A proper app structure

    - Use SingleChildScrollView to allow vertical scrolling.
3. Layout organization
    - Use a combination of layout widgets such as:
    - Column, Row, Container, Padding, Align, Center, or Expanded.
4. Content Elements
    - Use as many (Text, icons, images) as you need.
